LITTLE WORLD by Mr.Brian Design, embodies a "GOOD STYLE is GOOD SMILE" ethos within a fashionable character collection that celebrates youth culture's diversity and encourages a "Live Your BestLife" mindset. Rooted in tactile, fine art collages and showcased globally in digital art exhibitions, LITTLE WORLD began as a 3D character brand in web3, offering engaging experiences across digital collectibles, gaming, AR/XR, immersive art, stationery, and educational media, and creating collectibles like physical trading cards for a worldwide audience, while its game avatars have captivated discerning gamers.
